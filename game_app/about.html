About This Game

Week in Hanoi: Carbon Quest is a lightweight, client-only web game that helps you explore how daily choices affect CO₂ emissions. Play through 5 days, make eco-friendly decisions, and invest your earned coins in city improvements.

How CO₂ Is Estimated

  • Transport, food, and energy choices apply approximate CO₂ values.
  • Weather may modify outcomes (e.g., rainy day motorbike penalty).
  • Smog days add a small baseline for the day.

Team & Credits

Designed for RMIT Hackathon 2025. Illustrations and icons are simplified for performance. All logic runs in your browser—no backend.
